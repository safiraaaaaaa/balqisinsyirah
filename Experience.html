Experiences

Charming Boutique

After completing my SPM, I worked at Charming Boutique while waiting for my UPU results. My responsibilities included managing clothing stock, ironing clothes, assisting customers, handling cashier duties, and performing other related tasks. This was not my first working experience, but it still took me some time to adapt to a new working environment. Fortunately, my employer was kind and supportive, and I continued working there until I resigned to further my studies. Additionally, I was able to purchase my first mobile phone using the salary I earned from this boutique job, which made the experience even more meaningful to me.

During the ENT300 subject, my friends and I chose to sell ramen as our business project. Internship

I did my internship at the District Education Office (PPD) Johor, I was exposed to a professional working environment involving various administrative and information management tasks. My responsibilities included data entry using Excel, document handling and verification, record keeping, and assisting in the preparation and execution of official events. Through these tasks, I developed skills in organisation, accuracy, time management, and professional communication. Working closely with officers and staff also enhanced my understanding of teamwork, office procedures, and the role of PPD Johor in supporting schools and educational administration within the district.
